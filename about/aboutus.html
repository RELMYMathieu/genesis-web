ABOUT US

SCROLL FOR MORE

GENESIS IS A RO-SPACE MANUFACTURER COMPANY DELIVERING RELIABLE AND CHEAP LAUNCH SERVICES TO LOW EARTH ORBIT AND BEYOND IN THE MOST REALISTIC STYLE. 

Genesis was founded under the belief that realism is a key component within a modern ro-space group, here at Genesis Space, we have 2 main goals, realism and assets quality details.

As a new Ro-Space group, we aim to deliver players a realistic experience in every aspect and grow our company into one of the biggest Ro-Space groups.

Access to space is a key factor in the modern age, here at Genesis space, we aim to deliver our customers a cheap, reliable and fast way to launch spacecrafts and satellites into any type of orbits without any issues.
      
When designing a vehicle, that specific vehicle needs to follow 3 key rules: Reusability, Fast Launch Cadence Option, Simplification

These 3 rule keys allow a launch vehicle to be a truly modern rocket.

This is the path that we chose to do with Astraeus, our first rocket.

If you want to learn more about Astraeus, you can check our "Space Systems" tab.
      
Genesis Space is currently developing a new series of next-generation engines, Full Flow Stage Combustion cycle engines, running on Liquid Methane, also referred to as Natural Gas and Liquid Oxygen.

Our next-generation engines provide a huge boost in our launch vehicles operations, these engines are the key to a future within the space industry.
