Genesis Space

Genesis Space was founded under the belief that there should be a future where all vehicles are reusable and capable of going beyond our home planet, without incurring a huge cost price in a cost-effective manner. In order to achieve this, we need to recieve opinions from a healthy community, and it is the job of the Moderator to ensure that this community remains free of anti-social elements and individuals working against our organization.

Moderator

The moderator is responsible for regulating the propagation of content in our communication services between the organization and the community and arbitrate over disputes between individuals. The moderator shall recieve the authority to perform moderative actions within our communication services, including but not limited to muting, kicking and banning, and he/she is expected to utilise that authority responsibly.

As a moderator, you report to the Human Resources department, should there be any serious issue that requires oversight by higher authorities. However, you are expected to resolve most situations on your own.

One of the main priorities at Genesis, is to ensure that our staff are in a good condition and provide a suitable environment to foster productivity in individuals. To ensure this, you are expected to be honest about any problems that you may face as an employee of Genesis. If you face any consequences because of misconduct by one or more employees, you are expected to report these issues to any rank higher than Moderator in the HR department. The HR Department will initiate a investigation based on your claims, and should it be determined that misconduct has taken place, the HR department shall take actions necessary to create a healthy work environment.

Should you face any problem as a newcomer within in the company, members in the Human Resources Department are readily available to support you and resolve your queries.

Responsibilities

  • Engage in casual discussions with the community to foster a friendly environment for newcomers into the organization
  • Prevent members from propagating violent ideas, creating illogical and ad-hominem arguments, and take necessary actions to restore a friendly environment in a communication channel.
  • Participate and collaborate with other individuals within the department
  • Avert any future issues that may affect the company in any way
  • Attempt to assist staff members and new individuals in the company

Basic Qualifications

  • Be involved in Genesis Space and engage frequently with the community for 3 months
  • 1 year of playing the platform Roblox.
  • You must be above 13 years old.
  • Proficiency in the understanding of the features provided by Discord and our communication services

Additional Requirements

  • You may be asked to remain active for a prolonged period of time should we face any threat of a server raid.

Incentives to work at Genesis Space

In the future, we will be paying all Moderators within Genesis Space, the payment will be based on your efforts and how the community percieves it.

As of right now, applying for this job allows you to have a lot of positive benefits, a key benefit is that you can meet new and smart people in the team with who you can work together flawlessly.

As a Moderator, you are eligible for long-term Robux dividends, meaning you can receive a long-term Robux award up to 500 Robux depending on how well you do throughout half a year period.
