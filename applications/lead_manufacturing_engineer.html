Genesis Space

Genesis Space was founded under the belief that there should be a future where all vehicles are reusable and capable of going beyond our home planet, without incurring a huge cost price in a cost-effective manner. In order to achieve this goal, the Lead Manufacturing Engineer is a vital component to lead our engineers within the staff team.

Lead Manufacturing Engineer

The Lead Manufacturing Engineer (abbreviated "LME") acts as the leader and mentor of all engineers within the Manufacturing Department of the Genesis staff team. It is a valued and respectable role that requires traits and skills of communication, loyalty, sense of respect, wisdom through experience and education.

At Genesis Space, we value wise minds and brilliant creators. Therefore, it is one of our priorities to implement proper work ethics and a pleasant work environment. The Lead Manufacturing Engineer is expected to contribute to this priority by ensuring that such ethics are being properly enforced within the Manufacturing Department. The LME should also facilitate communication between different departments within Genesis Space to ensure proper growth of the organization.

Responsibilities

  • Develop highly reliable and scalable infrastructure for Genesis to be used throughout all space centers
  • Build prototypes to allow us to further our advance into final goal.
  • Control all aspects of the Manufacturing Department.
  • Voice your opinion in meetings held to decide the future of the company.
  • Dive into problems faced by staff members and decide on the best solutions.
  • Lead a team of ingenious minds and engage in collaborative efforts to bring best results.

Basic Qualifications

  • 1 year of working on the platform Roblox Studio.
  • 1 year of playing the platform Roblox.
  • You must be above 14 years old.
  • You must have a basic understanding of Physics.

Additional Requirements

  • You may be asked to work for an extended time period depending on different launch and testing cadences.

Incentives to work at Genesis Space

In the future, we will be paying all Engineers within Genesis Space, the payment will be based on your tasks and how fast you finish them.

As of right now, applying for this job allows you to have a lot of positive benefits, a key benefit is that you can meet new and smart people in the team with who you can work together flawlessly.

As an Engineer, you are eligible for long-term Robux dividends, meaning you can receive a long-term Robux award up to 1k Robux depending on how well you do throughout half a year period.
