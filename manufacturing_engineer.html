Genesis Space

Genesis Space was founded under the belief that there should be a future where all vehicles are reused and that are capable to go beyond Low Earth Orbit without a huge cost price, in order to achieve this goal, we need a key component within a regular staff team, and that is Manufacturing Engineers.

Manufacturing Engineer

The Manufacturing Engineer team is the central staff team of Genesis Space - the awesome team within the Engineering Department allows us to accelerate vehicle production, Genesis infrastructure, etc.

We are looking forward for as many engineers as possible that have 3 neccessary skills in mind, fairness, respect and support, without these 3 skills in mind, no one can achieve something.

Genesis Space has a different approach with the applications, here, we do not look for people that have past Ro-Space experience, instead we look for people that are smart, motivated, respectful, collaborative and those who actually intend to work full-time.

You will be working with an awesome team made out of smart engineers, where the communicating and team-helping skills are neccessary.

Responsibilities

  • Develop highly reliable infrastructure for Genesis that are used throughout all space centers.
  • Build prototypes that allow us to further advance into our final goal.
  • Own all aspects of Manufacturing Engineering.
  • Bring you voice to the table in team collaborations on architecture, design and build reviews.
  • Deep dive into your users' problems to find and create efficient solutions.

Basic Qualifications

  • 1 year of working on the platform Roblox Studio.
  • 1 year of playing the platform Roblox.
  • You must be above 14 years old.
  • You must have a basic understanding of physics.

Additional Requirements

  • You may be asked to work for an extended time period depending on different launch and testing cadences.

Incentives to work at Genesis Space

In the future, we will be paying all Engineers within Genesis Space, the payment will be based on your tasks and how fast you finish them.

As of right now, applying for this job allows you to have a lot of positive benefits, a key benefit is that you can meet new and smart people in the team with who you can work together flawlessly.

As an Engineer, you are eligible for long-term Robux dividends, meaning you can receive a long-term Robux award up to 1k Robux depending on how well you do throughout half a year period.
